### Life at Anceu – The Practical Side

Getting to Anceu is straightforward whether you’re traveling by car or public transport. You’ll find all the detailed information in our [travel guide](https://anceu.notion.site/How-to-get-to-Anceu-Coliving-2c55b7da5b384104a3fb09f0eedb87a6?pvs=4).

When it comes to food, we’ve created a system that brings the community together while respecting everyone’s independence. Your stay includes 24/7 access to coffee, tea, and bread for breakfast. We organize **family dinners Monday, Wednesday, and Friday** – they’re optional to join at 4€ per day. For groceries, we do weekly online orders where you can add both your personal items and contribute to communal dinners. If you have a car, there’s also a supermarket just 10 minutes away.

Your work setup is well taken care of with our 1Gbps fiber connection. You’ll find spaces to match your work style – **silent coworking areas, call-friendly spaces, and dedicated rooms for private meetings or calls**.

Daily life here is comfortable and hassle-free. **We handle the cleaning of all shared spaces**, and your utilities are all included. Need to do laundry? We have two washing machines and a dryer available for 3€ per load, with all cleaning products included.

Life in Anceu goes beyond just working remotely. You’ll find yourself naturally **becoming part of our village life**, whether through joining **local impact projects**, exploring the hiking trails, or enjoying the peaceful rhythm of rural Galicia.

We’re here to support you throughout your stay, ready to help with recommendations, answer questions, or assist with anything you might need to feel at home in Anceu.

## Pricing

The prices are per night and the **minimum stay is 15 days**, with a **maximum stay of 60 days**. Discounts apply for longer stays (**\-30% for stays longer than 30 days**) There are **2 pricing periods**: high season (June 1st to September 30th) and low season (March 1st to May 31st, and October 1st to November 30th).

Price is for single occupancy. If 2 people stay in the room, we add 15 EUR/day to the base rate. Long stay discounts also apply to double occupancy.

## Payment and Cancellation policy

You pay **your first 15 nights when you make your booking** online. The rest, along with other extras like drinks/laundries, is paid at the end of your stay (or monthly if you stay more than 1 month). Payments can be made on site by wire transfer.

#### Cancellation policy

Bookings are not refundable
