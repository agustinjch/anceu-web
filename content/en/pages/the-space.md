Our space is located in Anceu, a small parish in Ponte Caldelas, Pontevedra, Galicia.

We have 11 rooms, a coworking space with high-speed internet, communal kitchen, and beautiful gardens.

Surrounded by nature, with hiking trails, rivers, and forests right at your doorstep.
