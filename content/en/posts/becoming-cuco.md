*Becoming Cuco* is not just a documentary; it’s a journey into the heart of creativity. It’s where the lines between human expression and artificial intelligence blur. This documentary chronicles the unique partnership between Cuco and Anis.

[Cuco, a renowned artist](http://cucoismy.name) celebrated for his distinctive style.

[Anis Sakkaf](https://www.instagram.com/dunny.patuny/), a visionary technologist driven by a passion for innovation.

## Rural Hacker Residencies

The Rural Hackers residencies are hosted by [Anceu Coliving](https://anceu.com/), and organised by [Rural Hackers](https://ruralhackers.com). They serve as a beacon for innovators, thinkers, and creators seeking inspiration and connection in the heart of the countryside. From artists and technologists to researchers and entrepreneurs. The Rural Hackers residencies attract individuals from around the globe, united by a passion for innovation and exploration.

During the [Rural Hackers residencies](https://anceu.com/rural-hackers-residencies-2/), participants have the opportunity to delve into their creative pursuits. Whether it’s developing new artistic techniques, prototyping, or exploring projects at the intersection of art, science, and society. With access to state-of-the-art facilities, including coworking spaces, studios, and maker labs, residents are empowered to bring their ideas to life in an environment that fosters creativity and collaboration. Moreover, the residencies provide a platform for residents to engage with the local community, exchanging ideas, skills, and perspectives while contributing to the cultural fabric of Anceu and beyond.

## A collaborative process

The Rural Hackers residency thrives on collaboration, uniting artists and technologists in a quest for creativity and innovation. In “Becoming Cuco,” Cuco’s artistic vision and Anis’s tech expertise intertwined in a unique dance. The residency aimed to train AI, under Anis’s guidance, to echo Cuco’s style. Over weeks, they honed algorithms, capturing Cuco’s essence.

## Adding the artworks into the forest

As the residency progressed, Cuco and Anis arrived at a pivotal moment: the creation of the final pieces. Armed with a refined AI model and a deep understanding of Cuco’s artistic sensibilities, they embarked on a collaborative journey to bring their vision to life. Both Cuco and the AI were given the same prompt, inviting them to interpret it through their distinct lenses. The result was a breathtaking juxtaposition of human and machine creativity, each piece bearing the imprint of its creator while showcasing the symbiotic relationship between art and technology.

With the final pieces completed, the next chapter of the collaboration unfolded in the tranquil embrace of the forest surrounding Anceu Coliving. Against the backdrop of nature’s brightness, Cuco and Anis installed the artworks, transforming the forest into an immersive gallery space. This symbolic gesture not only celebrated the culmination of their collaborative journey but also laid the groundwork for future explorations at the intersection of art, technology, and nature. As visitors traverse the forest path adorned with these digital and analog artworks, they are invited to contemplate the boundless possibilities of human creativity and the transformative potential of collaborative endeavours.

## Conclusion

The journey documented in *Becoming Cuco* offers a captivating glimpse into the evolving landscape of creativity in the digital age. Through their collaborative efforts, Cuco and Anis have pushed the boundaries of artistic expression. They have blurred the lines between human ingenuity and artificial intelligence. As the residency comes to a close, the project leaves us with more questions than answers, inviting us to ponder the implications of AI-driven creativity in the realm of art.

The emergence of AI as a tool for artistic expression raises profound questions about the nature of creativity and the role of technology. *Becoming Cuco* offers a glimpse into the possibilities of AI-assisted artistry. It also prompts us to consider the ethical and philosophical implications of this field. As artists and technologists continue to explore the potential of AI in creative fields, we are faced with complex questions about authorship, authenticity, and the very nature of human creativity.

Ultimately, *Becoming Cuco* challenges us to embrace the unknown and embrace the transformative potential of collaborative exploration. As we navigate the ever-shifting terrain of art and technology, may we remain open to the possibilities that lie ahead, guided by the spirit of curiosity, innovation, and boundless creativity.
