Life at Anceu moves at a different pace. The morning starts with the sound of birds rather than alarms.

The coworking space fills up gradually as people from all over the world settle in for a day of focused work. Coffee brews, conversations start in hushed tones, and by mid-morning the energy is palpable.

Lunch is shared in the communal kitchen. Someone is making pasta, another is trying a local recipe. The ingredients are always fresh, always local.

Evenings are for walks in the forest, trips to the river, or simply sitting on the terrace watching the sunset over the hills.

This is what coliving looks like in rural Galicia.
