Anceu Coliving is a rural coworking and coliving space in Spain, offering a unique living and working experience for remote workers. The space is nestled in the beautiful Galician countryside, with breathtaking views of the natural surroundings. Anceu Coliving aims to bring people back to rural areas and create a sense of community among remote workers.

The coliving space offers a range of accommodations, including shared and private rooms, coworking spaces, and call rooms. In addition to work-related amenities, Anceu Coliving has communal spaces for socializing and relaxing, such as the cafe, kitchen, and chill area.

Anceu Coliving organizes a range of activities for remote workers, providing opportunities for community building and local exploration. The in-house activities include skill sharing sessions, movie nights, and board games, while the out-of-house activities include hikes, beach days, and visits to creative spaces in the area. Anceu Coliving has a strong focus on community building and local impact, making it the perfect place for remote workers seeking a unique experience in rural Spain.

The main objective of Anceu Coliving is to bring people back to rural areas and to create a sense of community among remote workers. This is achieved through the range of amenities and activities offered by the space. The coworking spaces are fully equipped with all the necessary facilities for a productive workday, including high-speed internet, comfortable desks and chairs, and ample natural light. Call rooms are also available for remote workers who need to make phone calls or hold virtual meetings.

## Community

One of the main benefits of Anceu Coliving is the sense of community that it provides. Unlike traditional accommodation options, Anceu Coliving offers the opportunity to connect with like-minded individuals. Those individuals, share a passion for remote work and a desire to create a positive impact in the local community. Colivers also have access to impactful local projects. For example, communal mural painting and [“Entre Culturas”](https://anceu.com/entre-culturas-event/) events. These allow remote workers and locals to connect and learn from each other through food and culture.

In addition to providing a unique community and impactful local projects, Anceu Coliving is committed to sustainability. The space recycles plastic, glass, and paper, and gives food waste to a neighbor for their pigs. They also use no chemical cleaning or washing machine products. Anceu Coliving also works with a local NGO, [A Rente do Chan](https://arentedochan.org/), to promote environmental sustainability in the area.

Anceu Coliving has quickly gained a loyal following of returnee colivers, with a return rate of around 55%. As a Rural coworking and coliving in Spain, Anceu Coliving aims to encourage remote workers to move to the Galician countryside and create more impact in the area. The space has already inspired a few people to move to the area, including one person to Anceu, one person to Ponte Caldelas, and three people to Pontevedra.

Anceu Coliving offers a unique and inspiring coliving experience that combines community, impactful local projects, and sustainability. It is the perfect place for remote workers seeking a one-of-a-kind living and working experience in rural Spain.
